## Overview

**Voice_Link** is an immersive, real-time voice transcription application that combines modern web speech technologies with a distinctive cinematic aesthetic inspired by the Silent Hill video game series.

## What is Voice_Link?

Voice_Link transforms your voice into text in real-time, displaying it through a unique CRT-style interface that mimics vintage computer terminals. The application is designed to be both functional and visually engaging, creating an atmospheric experience while serving as a practical accessibility tool.
