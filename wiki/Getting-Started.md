# Getting Started with Voice_Link

This guide will help you get started with Voice_Link in just a few minutes.

## Quick Start (No Installation Required)

The fastest way to use Voice_Link is through the web:

1. **Open the Live Demo**: [https://anacondy.github.io/Silent-Hill-Transcriber/](https://anacondy.github.io/Silent-Hill-Transcriber/)
2. **Allow Microphone Access**: Click "Allow" when prompted
3. **Start Recording**: Click the microphone button to begin transcription
4. **Speak**: Your words will appear on screen in real-time
5. **Copy**: Click the copy button to save your transcript

## System Requirements

### Minimum Requirements
- **Browser**: Chrome 90+, Safari 14+, or Edge 90+
- **Microphone**: Any working microphone (built-in or external)
- **Internet**: Required for speech recognition (Google's servers)

### Recommended
- **Browser**: Latest Chrome or Safari
- **Display**: Any resolution (responsive design)
- **Audio**: Good quality microphone for best accuracy

## Browser Permissions

Voice_Link needs microphone access to transcribe your speech. Here's how to enable it:

### Chrome
1. Click the lock icon in the address bar
2. Select "Site settings"
3. Set "Microphone" to "Allow"

### Safari (macOS)
1. Go to Safari → Settings → Websites
2. Click "Microphone" in the left panel
3. Find the site and select "Allow"

### Safari (iOS)
1. Go to Settings → Safari → Camera & Microphone Access
2. Toggle on microphone access

### Edge
1. Click the lock icon in the address bar
2. Select "Permissions for this site"
3. Set "Microphone" to "Allow"

## Your First Transcription

1. **Navigate** to Voice_Link
2. **Wait** for the interface to load (you'll see "System Standby...")
3. **Click** the microphone button (it will turn green)
4. **Speak** clearly into your microphone
5. **Watch** as your words appear in real-time:
   - Bright green text = interim (thinking)
   - Muted olive text = final (confirmed)
6. **Copy** your transcript using the copy button
7. **Stop** recording by clicking the microphone again

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Speak Clearly**: Enunciate words for better accuracy
2. **Reduce Noise**: Use in a quiet environment
3. **Use Chrome**: Best speech recognition accuracy
4. **Stay Close**: Keep microphone at consistent distance
5. **Pause Between Sentences**: Helps with punctuation
